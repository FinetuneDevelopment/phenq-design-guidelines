What is Ketosis?

Ketosis is a natural metabolic state. It involves the body producing ketones from your fat, and burning them for energy instead of carbs. This means your body will naturally use the stored fat in your body for energy, reducing your cravings for carbs and sugary foods.

Hemp protein contains 20 amino acids of which 9 are essential - meaning our bodies cannot make them by itself. It’s full of healthy omega fatty acids and fibre. It has also been shown to improve symptoms of fatigue and boost the immune system.

Brown rice

Brown rice is packed with antioxidants and nutrients to support weight loss and muscle building. Not only does it contain branched chain amino acids that are needed to build and repair muscle and tissue. It also supports your heart and liver function.
