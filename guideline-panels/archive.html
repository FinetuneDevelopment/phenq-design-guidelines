Quote disks

Quote disks are always circular and take the width of their container, so their size is controlled by the grid and not the contents.

The size of the text inside is entirely controlled by modifying classes and if the content grows too large for the container, this text will be clipped. The opening quote is attached to the top left corner of the content so will start to clip out of the circle before the content does.

Background colours are also controlled via a modifying class. By default, quote disks have no colour.
